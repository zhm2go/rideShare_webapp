User Guide


1 Create Account

Click 'Sign Up' at the upper right corner.

2 Login

Click 'Log in' at the upper right corner.

3 Login Failure

Invalid user name or password will not be able to log into system.

4 Logout

After login, click on the dropdown menu at the upper right corner, then click 'Log out'.

5 Register driver

Click on 'Become a Driver' in the navigation bar.

6 Edit driver & personal information

After becoming a driver, click 'Driver Info' in the navigation bar, then click edit or 'no longer be a driver' to modify driver information.

To change user information, click on the dropdown menu at the upper right corner, then click 'My account'.

7 Ride request

Click on the 'Call a Ride' in the navigation bar.

8 View non-complete rides

click on the dropdown menu at the upper right corner, then click 'All my called rides'.

9 Edit open ride

Follow the step as 8, then click 'edit' or 'delete' in the rightmost column.

10 Edit attributes

Follow the steps as 9.

11 View ride details

Follow the step as 8, if other people share the ride, click on 'ckeck here for share details' to view sharer's information. If a driver confirmed the ride, click on 'driver details' for driver information.

12 View all ride details

Follow the steps as 11.

13 Search open ride

Click on 'Share other's ride' in the navigation bar, then fill the form. The system will show all your request of sharing other's ride. Select the share request you want to process, click on 'search open rides'

14 Join a ride

Follow the steps as 13, click on 'join the ride'.

15 Driver searches requests

User must register to be a driver first. Then click on the 'Confirm an Order' in the navigation bar.

16 Confirm requests

Follow the step as 15, then click on 'confirm'.

17 Send email

After driver confirmed, system will automatically send the email.

18 List of confirmed ride

Click on the 'Complete an order' in the navigation bar.

19 Confirmed ride details

Follow the step as 18. If the ride is shared, click on 'check here for details' to view shared information.

20 Complete the ride

Follow the step as 18. Click on 'complete' to finish the ride.
